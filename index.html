Advanced · ICE servers (STUN / TURN)

Public STUN is enough for many home-to-home paths. With VPN, corporate NAT, or symmetric NAT, add a TURN entry, e.g. {"urls":"turn:turn.example.com:3478","username":"u","credential":"p"}. Free tiers: Metered, Twilio, Open Relay Project.
